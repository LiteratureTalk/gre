Act Belong Commit

Your pathway to positive mental wellbeing

Our Mission

Act Belong Commit is a mental health promotion campaign that encourages people to take action to improve their mental wellbeing. Developed in Western Australia, this framework provides a simple yet powerful approach to maintaining and improving mental health through three interconnected pillars.


The campaign aims to promote positive mental health in the community and reduce stigma by encouraging individuals to engage in activities that enable and build connection, purpose, and personal growth. By following these three simple principles, absolutely anyone can take proactive steps toward better mental wellbeing.

🎯

Act

Do something. Keep active mentally, physically, socially, and spiritually.

  • Take a daily walk or jog in nature
  • Learn a new skill or hobby
  • Practice mindfulness or meditation
  • Dance to your favorite music
  • Try gardening or cooking new recipes
  • Play sports or exercise regularly
🤝

Belong

Do something with someone. Build connections and foster relationships.

  • Join a community group or club
  • Volunteer for a local charity
  • Attend social events and gatherings
  • Connect with family and friends regularly
  • Join online communities of interest
  • Participate in team sports or group activities
💫

Commit

Do something meaningful. Take on challenges that provide purpose.

  • Set and work towards personal goals
  • Mentor someone or share your skills
  • Support a cause you believe in
  • Take on leadership roles
  • Learn about topics you're passionate about
  • Create art, music, or writing projects

Benefits of Act Belong Commit

🧠 Improved Mental Health

Reduce stress and anxiety levels

😊 Enhanced Mood

Increase happiness and life satisfaction

🌟 Greater Purpose

Find meaning and direction in life

🤗 Stronger Connections

Build lasting relationships and support networks

💪 Increased Resilience

Better cope with life's challenges

🎯 Personal Growth

Develop new skills and confidence
